His Excellency Dr. Mahamudu Bawumia congratulated the scholars for their First-Class achievements going through the awarded certificates. Urging the scholars to keep the bar for their careers higher. He pointed out that the engineering field is the hotspot for current economic development in countries in this day in age. Therefore, scholars should be initiative and innovative in their career approach, and should they need help in any shape and form, can reach out to his office for guidance.

His Excellency with the assistance of Ms. Saalai Manikam took the opportunity to distribute the certificates which His Excellency congratulate each in the process. A collective photograph was taken. Following the exit of His Excellency, the Scholars engaged in a detailed discussion on their current national service and their plans thereafter with Dr. Abudulai, Mr. Issahaku, and Ms. Saalai Manikam. With a lot of memories and advices to be shared, the session was continued over launch at Kohinnoor Indian restaurant and came to an end at 5: 17 PM.
